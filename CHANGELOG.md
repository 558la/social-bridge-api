# 更新日志 / Changelog  

## 2025-12-09  

**本次更新内容 / Updates:**  

- 上线 Instagram 注册接口，支持在系统内创建并注册新的 Instagram 帐号。  
  - Added Instagram account registration endpoint to create and register new Instagram accounts within the system.  
- 上线 Instagram 博主采集接口，可抓取指定博主的归档文章、图文等内容数据。  
  - Added Instagram creator collection endpoint to fetch posts and media from specified influencers.  
- 上线 Instagram 粉丝采集接口，用于获取帐号的粉丝列表及相关信息。  
  - Added Instagram followers collection endpoint to retrieve followers lists and related information.  
- 新增支持瑞典等冷门国家帐号，扩展更多国家地区的帐号注册与管理能力。  
  - Added support for accounts from Sweden and other niche countries, extending account registration and management capabilities.
